How We Work

Experience our comprehensive education consultancy model, where students receive personalized guidance in every facet of their US/HK/Singapore application(s). From strategic university selection and test preparation to expert support with personal statements/essays, extracurricular mentoring, and interview practice, we cover it all. Here’s how we work!
01: Building your plan
02: Choosing your schools
03: Acing your tests
04: Boosting your ECLs
05: Writing your essays
06: Nailing the interview
Slide 1 of 6

  • 01. Crafting a plan and getting started

    After your initial assessment with one of our Academic Advisors we will build a highly personalised plan based on your age, current situation and higher education admissions goals. Next you'll be introduced to your Educational Consultant who'll help you organise your entire Crimson journey and act as a regular communication base for you and your family.

  • 02. Building your university application list!

    Your Education Consultant is the 'captain of your application ship' helping you build out a **personalised application list **and formulate a highly personalised roadmap. Your consultant will help you pull all elements of your application(s) together, ensuring your final submission(s) will be as perfect as they can be. Read some of our education consultant profiles here!

  • 03. Lifting your standardised test scores

    Whether it be the SAT or ACT, AP or UK course specific exams, out tutors are standardised test top scorers who can help you lift your scores above the competition. As a Crimson student you'll have access to the Crimson App where you can organise tutoring sessions, access practice tests and get instant expert feedback.

  • 04. Improving your extracurricular candidacy

    Your ECL or extracurricular and leadership profile is a key component of your US and/or UK application(s). Our ECL mentors, who are accomplished project builders and often Ivy League or Oxbridge grads themselves, will help you expand upon your passions to build a capstone project and/or other significant, high-impact initiative.

  • 05. Working with your essay mentor

    Working with your essay mentor you will brainstorm, ideate, craft, edit and refine a personal statement that resonates with admissions officers. Our mentors are top school graduates who understand the nuances of what it takes to write a stand-our personal statement or supplemental essay.

  • 06. Prepping with your interview expert

    Our interview mentors have sat in that interview seat before - at Oxford, Cambridge and other top schools in the UK and the US. They will work with you to make sure you're prepared for every question possible, have the skill and confidence to think on your feet, and help you prepare questions you can ask your interviewers in return.
