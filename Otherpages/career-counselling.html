What is Career Counselling?

Career counselling is the process in which you get to explore and recognize your interests and preferences. It helps you understand how making a large personal change like choosing to move abroad to study could impact your future choices and career goals.

Career Counselor, Dr. Karan Gupta will guide you through face-to-face or online career counselling for those looking for advice and guidance on any life decisions that often go hand-in-hand with career planning. Karan Gupta is a Certified Career Counsellor (University of California, San Diego 2015), MBTI® Practitioner (2015), and also certified to conduct the MBTI®, MBTI Type II®, Strong®, MMTIC®, and FIRO®, DAT assessments and interest inventories which are taken by over a million students. He is India’s leading career counsellor and can help you with your career development, career choice and clear any confusion you have regarding your career decision.

An online career guidance and counselling session with Dr. Karan Gupta can reveal the entrance exams that you need to get admission to courses, which in turn will help you choose the right career. In this counselling session, you will be presented with various career options and career assessment tests through which your personality traits, interests abilities and long term career path can be discovered.

FAQs

When should I call you to book an appointment?

You should give us a call at least a month in advance to book the appointment. As soon as we receive your appointment request, we will find the earliest date available and fix the appointment for you.

Who will the appointment be with?

The career counselling session will be with Dr. Karan Gupta.

What will be covered in the initial appointment?

In the initial counselling session, Dr. Gupta will access your career path, your profile, what you have completed, and then based on your interests, preferable options will be recommended. He will discuss options like overseas education and study in India too.

If you are looking to study abroad, there will be information provided on different countries, their entrance exams, their application requirements and also the visa procedures.

If you are planning to change your career fields, Dr. Gupta will go through your current profile and depending on your choices, he will recommend how you can go about achieving your future goals.

If you wish to study in India, after thoroughly evaluating your profile, Dr. Gupta will recommend a few universities and colleges you can apply to for the course of your choice.

All appointments will include an evaluation of a complimentary psychometric test that the student has to attempt ahead of time.

Is the first appointment chargeable?

Yes, the initial appointment is chargeable since Dr. Gupta will be conducting a personal 1-on-1 session with you and all your queries will be discussed in this meeting. If you have any query even after the initial meeting, you can post messages on our portal.

Who is the psychometric test meant for?

The psychometric tests are suitable for anyone who wants to gain more insight into their likes and dislikes, their interests and preferences. All these factors come together to make you more aware of your current state and how you can improve accordingly in the future. Anyone above the age of 12 years can take the MBTI, Pearson DAT and Strong. Students below the age of 12 years can take the MMTIC.

Can I do the counselling session online or in person? I live out of station.

You have the option to do counselling in person, online or via telephone. Any method that you are comfortable with, we are more than happy to oblige.
