What Is Career Guidance?

In an ideal world, we would all start our working lives in our ideal job. A job that allows us to grow and learn, one that challenges and rewards in equal measure, and one that we enjoy and that brings us fulfillment – a passion.

If you are lucky enough to be in that position then most people will envy you! For the rest of us, career choice can seem difficult, and career change can seem impossible. Many people don’t have a deep understanding of their strengths and lack the self-knowledge to be confident in making important decisions about their future. This is where professional career counselling and life coaching can help. For working professionals, career development is a critical factor for achieving satisfaction at work. Don’t worry – Choosing the perfect career can be achieved with the right career tests and career guidance.

Career planning

Most students I meet don’t fully understand their career options because they don’t fully understand themselves and their interests. As a qualified career counsellor my priority is to get to know you, your career objectives, your goals, and aspirations, and what drives you. Then using a range of psychometric tests such as MBTI, STRONG, and Pearson DAT, I explore with you your true strengths, weaknesses, and motivations. Together we can then map out a personalized Career Exploration Program, helping you make a better career decision.

Understand your interests

Figure out what you are interested in, what career options excite you, and most importantly, why. Understanding why you like what you like will be the difference between thinking you like something and knowing you are genuinely interested.

Narrow down your field of choice

You should narrow it down to a particular field after due diligence. Thinking you find something assuming for a while and dedicating yourself to the subject matter can be very different experiences. You should research and work in a field before you devote yourself to the subject matter.

Fix an education pathway

Once you know what to do, you need to figure out how to do it. You must find out what degree you require to succeed in a career choice, how long it takes to finish the degree, any high education opportunities to sharpen your skills, or even how you can gain experience.

Academic performance and standardized tests

If you are planning to study abroad or applying to good institutes in India, at some point you will have to appear for standardized tests. The tests are often the only way to get into universities. Along with your test scores, the academic scores should also be equally good.

Extracurricular activities and recommendations

To stand out from your competition, you must have a strong profile and a good way to build a profile is to focus on the kind of experience you gain through working, internships, extracurricular activities, etc. You will also require recommendations from people who know you well and such opportunities will help you find the right recommenders.

Gaining experience

The easiest way to gain experience is to narrow down your field and find any options where someone from your background can be accepted. If you are in high school, you can find internships, part-time jobs, or do job shadows. Likewise, if you are in graduate school, you can explore the same options but also take a year or so to work in a field before you continue your studies.

Sometimes people find new passions, discover new careers, take a long gap, or realise later than most, that their line of work may not be fulfilling for them. In any case, I am here to help you figure out what you should do at any stage in your life be it your late 20s or late 50s. FAQs

How exactly will your services help me?

We start with a basic career test through which we will know your interests which helps us to narrow down the career fields ideally suited for you and suggest the path forward regarding all aspects of your career. Your interests, preferences, and goals are of the most important to us so Dr. Gupta will give you advice considering all these aspects.

Do I need career guidance if I know what I want to study or where I want to work?

There may be aspects of your career that you might be unaware of or you may not know hold huge importance. Adopting a holistic approach, through a career guidance session, Dr. Gupta will be to guide you on the various aspects of your career so that you can make an informed decision.

Why is career planning important?

Career planning is a long process that includes your education, work, and the various roles that you will play throughout your career and life. Hence, planning your career is vital for success in all areas of your life which leads to personal growth and development. The key is to pursue something which is fulfilling to achieve your life goals. This can be achieved through introspection and careful planning with the assistance of a certified career counsellor.

Is it too late to change my field?

Meeting a certified career counsellor is highly recommended to discuss all the possibilities of making a career switch as there are many factors to be considered which you might not be aware of.

How should I select my career?

Follow our Career planning strategy to methodically breakdown each section of your career plan and then find out what all you need to succeed at that stage and move on to the next step.
