Student Mentorship Program

The purpose of Mentorship Programs is to enhance the existing knowledge and skills of students from a young age. Ask yourself – What type of Mentoring Program or Mentorship Program would benefit you? Would a formal mentoring program with organized mentoring benefit you? A successful mentor is one who is a near-perfect role model. In the long term, a good mentor works toward the learning and development as well as specific skills of the mentee. Successful mentor-mentee relationships are those where the mentors and mentees are aligned to achieve the same goals and objectives. In the end, such a program works for the benefit of all the people involved.

Our Services

Career Guidance

In the Career Guidance section, you will study career exploration, careers of the future in STEM, Medicine, Business, Law, Communications, Civil Services and Arts which will help you narrow down the exact field that you are most suitable for. We understand that not everyone may be aware of what they would like to do in the future and these modules are designed to help you figure that out.

Profile Building 

The Profile Building sections goes in detail about what exactly a good profile has, guidance on the selection of school boards and subjects in high school, summer school programs, board exams prep and PSAT. This is extremely useful for students who are planning to study abroad in the top schools such as Harvard, Stanford, Cornell, Columbia, Princeton, Yale and so on, in the future.

Skill Development

The Skill Development section talks about how certain skills such as communication, time management, reading and setting goals can help you in the long run. Keeping in mind that the internet is a huge part of our lives, we have even included interesting topics like social media and how to use it to get the best outcome, how to become a digital influencer, how to deal with safety online and also becoming a social media influencer.

Mindfulness & Wellness

With so much information around you, it is normal to feel overwhelmed. With guidance from us and effort from you, student life will be a piece of cake. The section on Mindfulness and Wellness will get you prepared for challenges in college or even professional life. We focus on topics such as stress management, physical wellness and sports, food and nutrition, computer literacy, utilizing your brainpower, positive affirmation and personal growth.

Psychometric Testing

Our Psychometric Tests such as the MBTI, Strong and DAT will help you understand your personality, what comes naturally to you and what your strengths and weaknesses are. Once you do these tests, we will be able to guide you as to what subjects you should be selecting, how you should be studying, what majors you can pursue in college and how to maximize your potential.

Course Details

  • We understand that everyone is different and some of you may even be facing unique challenges compared to other students in your class. Considering that, we have made sure to include details for students who may be facing any kind of learning disabilities.
  • We have developed a step-by-step detailed module- based learning process that will help you keep track of your progress and make it easier for you to access information. Once you start the program with us, you will have access to over 27 online modules.
  • You can choose to attempt these modules the way you see fit – you can save your progress and return to the point you left in the previous session.
  • Each module consists of a video explaining a particular topic, reading material, and links to helpful websites, quizzes to help you understand the aim of the module and prioritise your game plan.
  • The entire program is online and you will have periodic, structured meetings with Dr. Karan Gupta, who will serve as your student mentor and maintain the mentoring relationship.
  • While our program participants are typically students from grades 6 onwards, if you believe you are ready to take a positive first step in self-improvement, you can start the program even earlier.

FAQs

Is there anything unique in this program?

This program is designed to be helpful to anyone who wants to explore ways to better their life across all spheres. We understand that everyone is different and some of you may even be facing unique challenges compared to other students in your class. Considering that, we have made sure to include details for students who may be facing any kind of learning disabilities.

How does the program work?

We have developed a step-by-step detailed module-based learning process that will help you keep track of your progress and make it easier for you to access information. Once you start the program with us, you will have access to over 27 online modules. Each module consists of a video explaining a particular topic, reading material, and links to helpful websites, quizzes to help you understand the aim of the module and prioritize your game plan.

Is there a sequence I need to follow?

You can choose to attempt these modules the way you see fit – you can save your progress and return to the point you left in the previous session.

Is the program done online or offline?

The entire program is online and you will have periodic, structured meetings with Dr. Karan Gupta, who will serve as your student mentor and maintain the mentoring relationship.

How old do I have to be to start this program?

While our program participants are typically students from grades 6 onwards, if you believe you are ready to take a positive first step in self-improvement, you can start the program even earlier.

Do I get any acknowledgment after I finish the program?

You will earn a Certificate of Achievement upon completion of the Student Mentorship Program and list it on your resume or CV.
