FAQs

Which tests do I need to give to study in the US?

To study in the US,you will have to give the TOEFL for your language requirement but depending on your course you will also have to appear for SAT/ACT(undergraduates), GRE(most postgraduates), GMAT(MBA), etc. 

When should I start preparing for SAT/ACT or GRE/GMAT?

To prepare for any standardized test, you need at least 3-4 months of preparation.

What is the minimum score the universities accept for IELTS?

The minimum requirement differs for all universities. For an average university, you should aim for a band 7 with no section below 6.5 individually. 

Which is better: IELTS or TOEFL? Do I need to give them before I start my applications?

You should give the IELTS if you want to apply to several universities in different countries. If you are focused on the USA and Canada only, you should give TOEFL. You should give the respective exam before you start your applications.

Can I get into a US university without giving a GRE or GMAT for my masters?

We highly recommend that you give the GRE or GMAT as per the specific course and university requirements.
