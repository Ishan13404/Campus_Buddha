FAQs

Which country should I study in?

The important factors to take into account are the course that you intend to pursue, the university offering the course, and the country it is located in. This will help you in narrowing down your choices. USA, UK, Canada, and Australia are popular choices amongst Indian students.

How many countries can I apply to?

There are no restrictions on the number of countries that you can apply to. The key is to narrow down your options according to your profile and preferences after evaluating all the possibilities.

When should I start my applications to study abroad?

Starting preparations for the application process at least a year in advance would be a good idea.

Do I need to apply for a visa first?

After you confirm your acceptance to the university by paying the deposit, the university will issue the necessary documents required for your visa application. Only after receiving these documents, you are allowed to apply for a student visa.

Do I need to be fluent in the local language to study in a country?

It is not necessary but preferable to start learning the local language as it will ease the process of adapting to a foreign country.
