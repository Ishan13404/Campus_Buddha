Why Study in India

India’s education system has grown immensely from the ancient times of studying in gurukulas to universities which produce some of the brightest minds in the world. We come from a culture of religious teachings intermingled with science and technology varying across the land in terms of the medium of instruction or even the ways we are taught. A myriad culture can perhaps be what we need to stand out in today’s developing world but it can be a huge task to figure out what we want to do in the future to succeed and that’s where Dr. Karan Gupta comes in.

FAQs

Will you get me admission in any university?

We do not have tie-ups with any universities as it would hinder us from being independent and unbiased counsellors. You will get admission based on your merit alone.

Will you fill my application forms?

We will not fill your application forms. We can give you information about admission and Dr. Karan Gupta will develop a plan of action for you that can help you with understanding when to do what in the admission process.

How can you help me in selecting a course?

In your initial career counselling session, we take a career inventory test. The results of this test are discussed in the meeting with Dr. Gupta. So after taking your interest, test reports and preference into account, you will know which courses you can select after your meeting with certainty.

Do you offer job placements?

We do not offer job placements and solely work on the education aspect of your career.
