Timeline for Admissions

Finalising university

Once you receive offer letters from different universities, you will have to fix the one university you want to go to. Consider the course they are offering, location and how it helps your future while deciding.

Apply for I-20(US only) or respective alternative for your country

Now that you know which university you will be studying at, you can request the university to send you a confirmation. This comes in the form of a letter which you need to start the visa process.

Apply for Visa

After receiving your I-20, you will be able to start your visa application. You need several documents like your I-20, financial papers, copies of transcripts, valid passport, etc. for this process. We can help you get organized and fill your application.

Visa interview (if applicable)

The US has an interview stage in their visa process but other countries do not generally have an interview as a part of their process. Some countries however will ask for an interview with the student depending on their application details. We can help you prepare for the same by doing mock interviews.

Receive visa and prepare for departure

After you receive the visa confirmation and your passport back, you should start making travel related preparations. Keeping luggage restrictions in mind, try to keep as many essentials with you as possible for the first few weeks.

FAQs

Can I apply directly for a student visa?

No.  After receiving the offer/acceptance letter and the I-20 (or other documents depending on your country) from the university, only then you can apply for a student visa.

What should I do to prepare for my applications?

After getting offer letters from universities, follow our step by step guide to understand what stages you will come across during the application process.

How many times will you help me apply for a visa if I get rejected?

We will help you apply for one visa application.  We do not guarantee visas but since 1999, our success rate has been 100%.

Which documents do you help with?

We will provide you with samples for all documents required for the application process like bank letters, asset statements, etc. and we will fill your application form. 

Do you help with interviews?

We will have brainstorming sessions with you and help you with mock sessions for your interviews.

Do you give guarantees for visas?

We do not guarantee visas but since 1999, our success rate has been 100%.

Do you have tie-ups with universities?

We do not have tie-ups with any universities as we are not agents. We recommend universities to our students after considering their profile and their personal preferences.

Will the visa application charges be included in your fees?

The charges for the visa application will be paid by you and are not included in the visa counselling fees.

Will you be preparing my documents for me?

We will provide proper guidelines and samples for all documents you may need throughout the process. We will not be writing, applying for or collecting any documents for you.

Will you be booking any appointment date I want for me?

The appointment schedule is completely dependent on the particular consulate you are applying through. We have no control over which date you get and for the same reason we recommend you apply for the visa 3 months before your academic term begins.
